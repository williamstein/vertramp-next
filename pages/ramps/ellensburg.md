# The Ellensburg Vert Ramp (from the 1990s)

Back in the 1990s there was an epic 12' tall, 24' wide, masonite vert ramp in Ellensburg, Washington.  

## Article

There's [a 2020 article](https://issuu.com/dailyrecord/docs/2020_kvl-summer_q3) about the ramp.
